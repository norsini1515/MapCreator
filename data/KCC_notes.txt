Koppen Class Classification
	- Temperature variation
	- Precipiation variation

1st letter-	Major Climate Group
6 major groups- A B C D E H
A (tropical humid, low lattitude, warm + wet)
B (warm+dry climates, high pressures, 
C (mild mid-latitude climates, mild winter, warm/hot summers)
D (server mid-latitudes, severe/cold winters)
E (polar, very high latitude, cold climates)
H (high land, really tall, climate controlled by elevation)

2nd letter- Precipiation
A C D can have f m w s
	f- wet all year
	m- monsoonal summers (very wet summer season)
	w- winter dry (dry winter season)
	s- summer is dry season

B can have W S
	W- deserts, extremely dry climates
	S- steppes, semi arid climates

E can have
	T- tundra (>=1 month above freezing)
	F- ice caps (all months has averages below freezing)
	
3rd letter- extra info about temperature
C D can have a b c d
	a- hot summers
	b- warm summers
	c- cool summers
	d- very cold winters

B can have h k
	h- hot desert or steppe
	k- cold desert or steppe
